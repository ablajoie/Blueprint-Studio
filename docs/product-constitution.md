# Product Constitution

1. Metadata is the source of truth.
2. Documentation is generated from connected design data.
3. Users own and can export their project data.
4. Features should be discoverable from the Solution Explorer in three clicks or fewer.
5. The application is local-first and collaboration-ready.
6. Salesforce concepts are mirrored where familiarity helps and improved where Setup creates friction.
7. Health checks guide users and remain configurable.
8. AI suggestions require user review and approval.
9. Design decisions and requirements remain traceable to affected artifacts.
10. Every shipped feature must make solution design faster, clearer, or safer.
